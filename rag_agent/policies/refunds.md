# Refund and Quality Dispute Policy

## Scope
This policy defines the conditions under which refunds are issued, the documentation requirements for quality-related disputes, and the categories carrying elevated refund risk.

## Refund Eligibility
Refunds are issued under the following conditions:

- **Item not delivered**: SKU was included in the order but not received by the customer, confirmed by delivery manifest discrepancy.
- **Quality failure**: Item delivered was visibly damaged, spoiled, or did not meet the quality standard at the time of delivery.
- **Incorrect substitution**: A substitution was applied that the customer did not consent to or that violated the substitution policy. Substitution violations are logged as policy exceptions in the substitution audit trail.
- **Weight discrepancy**: For weighted items (produce, deli, meat), actual weight delivered was more than 15% below the charged weight.

Refunds are not issued for customer preference changes made after delivery, items consumed in part before a quality complaint is filed, or items where the customer declined the offered substitution and accepted an empty slot.

## Quality-Sensitive Categories
The following categories have elevated refund risk and require additional quality verification at pick. See department SOPs for category-specific standards where available.

- **Produce**: Items must be free of visible bruising, mold, or decay. Pre-bagged produce must not contain damaged items.
- **Meat and seafood**: Must be within sell-by date at time of pick, not at time of delivery. A minimum of 2 days' remaining shelf life is required.
- **Dairy**: Must not be within 24 hours of sell-by date at time of pick.
- **Eggs**: Carton must be inspected for cracks before dispatch. Any cracked egg in the carton is grounds for replacement of the full carton.
- **Prepared foods and deli**: Must be prepared same-day. Prior-day items are not eligible for fulfillment.

## Refund Tiers and Authorizations
| Refund Value | Authorization Required |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Under $10 | Automated — no approval needed |
| $10–$50 | Customer service agent |
| Over $50 | Customer service supervisor |
| Over $150 | Operations manager |

## Documentation Requirements
All refund requests must be recorded in the order management system with:
- Order ID and affected SKU(s)
- Refund reason code
- Customer-submitted evidence (photo, if applicable) for quality disputes over $20
- Agent ID and timestamp of decision

Quality disputes involving high-risk categories (meat, seafood, prepared foods) must have photographic documentation attached before the refund is processed, regardless of value.

## Refund Rate Monitoring
Fulfillment centers with a rolling 30-day refund rate exceeding 4% of orders are placed on a quality improvement plan. Individual picker refund rates are reviewed weekly as part of performance management.
